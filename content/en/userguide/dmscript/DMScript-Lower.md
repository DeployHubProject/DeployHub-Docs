  Convert to lowercase. 
---

lower returns a lowercase version of the passed string.

**Usage:**

String.lower()

Or

lower(String)

**Returns:**

String

**Examples:**

set a = "Hello There";

echo $a.lower();

Result:

hello there

**See Also:**

upper()
